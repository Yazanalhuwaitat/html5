Privacy Policy

Farmgirl Flowers, Inc. (owner of FarmgirlFlowers.com) takes very seriously the private nature of your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we treat the information we collect when you visit and order from our Web site. Please read this notice carefully.

User Consent

By accessing or using Farmgirl Flowers, you agree to the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy, which is a part of the Farmgirl Flowers terms of service. You expressly consent to the processing of your Personal Information according to this Privacy Policy.

Personal Information

We collect your Personal Information voluntarily through Farmgirl Flowers in order to create your user profile and account, as well as to fulfill your orders. The types of personally identifiable information that may be collected in order to fulfill order requests include: full legal name, billing and shipping addresses, e-mail address, telephone number, and credit card information. In some instances, we may request that you submit information about other people. For example, if you order a gift arrangement to be sent directly to the recipient, we will ask for the recipient's name, address, telephone number, and email address. Additionally, we may collect any information that you voluntarily disclose, including Personal Information, and any postings, comments, forums, or blogs within the Farmgirl Flowers community. We will never sell, rent or share your personal information, including your e-mail address, with any third parties for marketing purposes without your express permission. We may share your personal information for the purpose of delivering our e-mail to you or as required by law.

Security

We are committed to protecting the security of your Personal Information. We use industry-standard security technologies and procedures to help protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Even though we have attempted to take all possible precautions, we cannot fully eliminate security risks associated with Personal Information.

If you have any question about this policy please contact us.
